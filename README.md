## Alphabet
<a id="Alphabet"></a>
It sounds silly to start from the alphabet, but just like it happens in englis it will happen in portuguese. We speak different because we don't have the same sounds;


While in english there are 5 vowels , just like in portuguese, there are also around 20 (**twenty**)  sounds including short sounds, long sounds, and diphthongs. Well portuguese is a whole lot easier, we have the same 5 vowels but only 7 remarkable sounds and the diphthongs are made usin the vowels we have.

## Pronouns
<a id="Pronouns"></a>

There are 6 pronouns in portuguese
 
 #### Singular
 1. Eu (I)
   - Eu sou alto
   - I am tall
 2. Tu (you)
   - Tu és alto
   - You are tall
 3. Ele/Ela (He/She)
   Ele;ela é altoalta
 #### Plural
 1. Nós (We)
 1. Vos (You)
 1. Eles/Elas (They)

But in living language, we barely use the second person.
